Travel Tips for Stockholm in December

What to Pack: Layers, warm coat, waterproof boots, hat and gloves. The temperature hovers around freezing, but the wind can make it feel colder.

Getting Around: The public transportation system (SL) is excellent. Consider getting a travel card for unlimited rides.

Budget: Stockholm is expensive, but you can save money by shopping at grocery stores for some meals and taking advantage of free museum days.

Best Experience: Don't miss fika culture - the Swedish tradition of coffee and pastries with friends. It's a wonderful way to experience local life.
